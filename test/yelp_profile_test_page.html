    $$ Beer, Wine & Spirits, Cafes, Tapas/Small Plates
    Læderstræde 32
    1204 København
    Denmark
    • Share review
    • Compliment
    11/28/2014
    1 check-in

    A cafe in the heart of Copenhagen and by that I mean very close to the stork fountains and strøget. The cafe is on a parallel street of Strøget and is good for an after shopping bite. It's a quite popular place because of its location, hence be prepared to struggle for a table.

    I have been multiple times here on different occasions and still can't say I love it. There are some things that are a bit slow, like ordering or trying to pay. The fact that the cafe is popular comes with the negative side that the service is slow due to the high amount of customers. You need to be patient or lucky!

    Regarding the food, well it's more or less the typical cafe menu with nachos, burgers, sandwiches etc. The portions are quite large but it's nothing too spectacular! I like the fact that in the summer you can sit outside and enjoy the passing people. The interior of the cafe is ok nice but the tables are a little too close to each other for my taste.

    It's a safe place to go when in the centre, however will never be one of my favourite hang outs!

  • Mahalle
    $ Coffee & Tea, Middle Eastern, Breakfast & Brunch
    Birkegade 6
    2200 København
    Denmark
    • Share review
    • Compliment
    11/27/2014
    1 check-in

    What you can love about Nørrebro is that there is such great variety of cultures, offering the perfect setting for a Lebanese cafe like Mahalle. It is a cute cafe close to Sankt Hans Torv which serves good food in mezze style. An alternative lunch/brunch place where instead of the typical cafe food you get hummus, olives, pita bread, spices etc.

    We were there for lunch time after 12:30 and we were not the only ones. Seemed like people have discovered this place for lunch. I had the mezze plate that included different kind of pastes and pita bread. This is a cold meal, nothing is warmed up apart from the haloumi cheese. It was really good and the portion was almost too large.

    The service was very friendly and the girl explained what we wanted to know about the menu. The food came out quite fast and we all enjoyed what we had ordered. We also had their home tea, which I found a bit too strong as a taste. The prices were on the cheap side considering average Danish prices, especially if one would share and order few mezze plates.

    The interior is really cute and I was surprised I didn't know about mahalle thinking that I live not too far from it. I am glad I came across Mahalle and I recommend it for an alternative brunch or lunch!

    1 check-in

    My first experience from Oslo's food/cafés was here. I always have been getting warned how expensive everything is so I had to go into yelp and try find something suitable! Well I must say it was not a bad choice!

    This place on daytime serves food which is more or less the typical cafe food, with burgers and salads. I had one of the salads that had grilled cheese and it was good. The salad came with bread and aioli for free on the side (generous portion of bread). Not a bad deal. And I also thought the price was ok. It was sth like 170nok.

    The place itself is very special. Filled with numerous colours and strange decorative things. I saw also the backyard which everybody has commented on when I was leaving, which does seem very cosy. What I didn't really like was how dark the whole place was as there were only few windows to the outside. And even though the salad was ok good, it was not something I would be going back to.

    So in general terms it was nice but not amazing! Good for lunch or coffee in a cosy colourful place.

  • NEIGHBOURHOOD - Organic Pizza & Cocktails
    $$ Pizza, Cocktail Bars, Italian
    Istedgade 27
    1650 København V
    Denmark
    • Share review
    • Compliment
    11/25/2014

    This place gives you a great experience to be remembered. And why is that? Because you probably have never had such pizza before! Compared to an average pizza, the dough is very very thin, it is actually almost non existing! It reminds me a bit of the Arabic pita bread. And why is that good? Well the balance between the ingredients is so good that it the doughs purpose in this case is to just keep them together! I loved it!

    The toppings are generous and very tasteful! And the pizzas are served in such a new and great way. I mean they always have something to squeeze on them (lemon, lime, orange) which the waiters recommend you to do for more fresh taste. And then the green stuff ( like rucola f.e.) is put on one corner to be spread out if wanted. Wow!! Delicious!!!! My special favourite from the 4 I tasted was the Christmas pizza with duck! A must try!

    The place was also very nice. However it didn't have any great chairs. They are all either benches or stools. No chairs, so if you re looking for a place to really have comfortable seating maybe this is not the one! However the setup of the interior was nicely arranged and I liked the colours and style. There is also a bar that offers organic cocktails. The one I tried with the egg-yolk was not my favourite but it was ok good. Not a big fan of egg-yolk in my cocktails anyways.

    In general terms a good place to spend an afternoon or evening with friends! It has large tables and can fit even big groups of people. And the pizzas are a new experience!

    1207 København K
    Denmark

    11/22/2014
    1 check-in

    So I finally visited tight and I must say I was not disappointed! I haven't been to the U.S.A. but I imagine the food and service to be exactly like this.

    The service was excellent. Most people that worked there spoke perfect English and they were not Danish. They came to ask us if everything was fine and made sure that we were satisfied. They also were good at proposing good wines and explained everything about their dishes.

    The food was good and it's all about large portions and meat! If you are a girl take the small portion as it will be enough! I had the ribs and they were delicious but large! They came also with delicious mashed potatoes that had garlic and some other spices. Very good! If you like meat it's a perfect place to get a good dish and not pay a crazy amount of money for it.

    The restaurant is located very centrally near strøget. I was also pleased about the indoor  environment. It was very warm and had nice seating areas with low lighting.

    I also arrived there alone as my friend was late and they made sure I would feel comfortable and that I had everything I needed! It was a perfect evening!

    11/21/2014
    1 check-in First to Review

    Perfect place for a Wednesday to enjoy some girl time as they have womans Wednesday and price for cakes is 20 dkk per piece (half price). The cakes are good and the cafe is cute.

    We tried this cafe with another yelper as we had heard about the woman's Wednesday! It's a cute small cafe near stefansgade. It's half under the ground and it's on a corner. The cafe is not large, probably in total there are 3 window bar tables and 4-5 low tables. There were not many people at 14:30, just us and another table. It was slightly cold as the door was constantly half open. But as a place it has nice colours and cute furniture.

    The service was friendly and the cakes were delicious. A bit too sweet for my taste. I couldn't finish my lemon pie as it was so sugary. So I would recommend to start with one cake shared between two and if there is room for second piece then you could take another one. I also had a warm chocolate for 35 dkk which was good.

    Cute cafe but I believe I will not be rushing back. A little more feeling was missing. Maybe candles on tables, some music, etc. but good for a Wednesday cake!!!!

  • Sticks ‘n’ Sushi
    $$$ Sushi Bars
    Gammel Kongevej 120
    1850 Frederiksberg
    Denmark
    • Share review
    • Compliment
    11/16/2014
    1 check-in

    This is my second sticks and sushi that I have been to and I must say the one in Tivoli is a bit better. However, I loved the food and it definitely is my favourite sushi chain in town.

    If you are a sushi lover I would recommend this place to you. The food is fresh and they have a great menu with amazing variety. It is a bit expensive but you pay for what you get! There are two menus, one where they have their whole food variety and one for the fixed menus. I find that the menu in Tivoli hotel was slightly easier to guide through and the fixed menu card had larger variety.

    The tables are a bit small and even though we were four they put us to a square table where we hardly could fit all the food. The service was very friendly and helpful but we had to wait quite a lot of time for the food to come out and then it came all at once, although we had ordered salads which could have come first.

    Generally a good experience with some room of improvement. Will go back for another sushi experience.

  • Risteriet Halmtorvet
    $ Coffee & Tea, Breakfast & Brunch, Tapas Bars
    Helgolandsgade 21
    1653 København
    Denmark
    • Share review
    • Compliment
    11/16/2014
    1 check-in

    An average cute cafe near the meat packing district. There is free wifi so you can find some loners at the window tables on their computers. On the bad side, the cafe does not have so many tables so be prepared for waiting for one or for squeezing in with others.

    The interior is quite rustic, which I liked. The walls are from bricks and there are small black lamps on top of every table. The coffee and warm chocolate that we had were good but again, I am not a coffee expert. I liked the music which was chilling and relaxing in the background. There was a good atmosphere in this cafe although it is halfway under the street level.

    The service was very friendly and fast and it seemed like a popular cafe on a Sunday afternoon. If you re in the area it's a good cafe to spend few hours at, but I don't think I would travel from another part of the city just to go there.
